WHAT WE ARE
Not an AI agency.
Not a consulting firm.
Not a software company.
tdvizh.ai is a Business Architecture company.

We study how a business actually runs, find where the structure breaks, and design the system that holds it together — before any technology enters the picture.

PHILOSOPHY
AI is a tool. Architecture is the foundation. Technology follows structure.

Automation without architecture doesn't fix a business — it only makes the chaos faster. So we start elsewhere: with how the company actually works, long before a single system gets built.

HOW WE WORK

Architecture before automation.

01

Review

We map how the business actually operates today — where information moves, where it stalls, and where a person is quietly compensating for a missing system.

02

Design

We redesign the process itself. A broken workflow, automated, is still a broken workflow — just a faster one.

03

Build

Only then do we choose the technology — AI agents, CRM architecture, automation — matched to the structure, not the other way around.

04

Evolve

The system keeps working after we leave. It's reviewed and extended as the business grows — architecture, not a one-time install.
